About

Catherine is a Web Developer at Sparkbox, a commercial web shop in Southwest Ohio, building responsive websites up and down the stack with technologies like CSS, Sass, JS, React, Craft CMS, PHP, Node, and more. She has a B.S.A. from Miami University in Art Education. While Catherine no longer teaches high school, her strong passion for education has never left; she spends a good amount of time volunteering with Girl Develop It Dayton, sitting on the leadership team and teaching code classes. Her other hobbies include reading, video games, tabletop games, oil painting, and making cat jokes. Twitter (and all the other things) as catheraaine.
