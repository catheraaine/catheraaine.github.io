About

Hello! I'm Catherine, and I build the internets.

I am an Apprentice Web Developer at Sparkbox, a commercial web shop in Southwest Ohio. I graduated from Miami University with a B.S.A. in Art Education, taught high school for a while, and decided that it wasn't for me. I spent nearly three years working for Cincinnati StartUp ChoreMonster before deciding to become a full-time developer.

That strong passion for education has never left me; I spend a good amount of time volunteering with Girl Develop It, teaching code classes and generally helping out. My other hobbies include reading, video games, tabletop games, oil painting, and boffer sports.
