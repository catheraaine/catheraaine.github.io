Die Mechaniker

Heavily armored and highly defensive, Die Mechaniker rely on survivng what their enemies throw at them.

Your starting HQ is always treated as Fortified when you defend it.

If your defense roll is two natural 6s, that territory cannot be attacked again for the rest of the turn.

Enclave of the Bear

Savage and primal, the Enclave of the Bear are genetically altered humans who terrify their enemies with their ferocity.

The defender subtracts 1 from her lower defense die in the first territory you attack during your turn.

If your attack roll is a natural three of a kind and at least one defending troop is defeated, you conquer the territory. Remove all defending troops.

Khan Industries

Well-armed but not well-trained, Khan Industries is overpopulated with members from all genetic backgrounds. Their motto: people and guns are cheap to make.

At the start of your turn, place one troop in each territory that has a HQ you control.

When drawing a territory card, you may place one troop into that territory if you control it.

The Saharan Republic

Savvy warriors who fight with old equipment and guerilla-like tactics, The Saharan Republic exists to be mobile and hard to find.

You can make your maneuver at any point during your turn.

When making your maneuver, you may maneuver between any two territories you control, even if they are not connected.
